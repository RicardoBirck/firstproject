About Me

Welcome to my page!

My name is Ricardo Birck, and I love philosophy and technology!

My background is in education and app development. From 2017 to 2022, I was the co-founder of an ed-tech that built a language learning app called Lingua Franca. My roles included setting the vision, developing the curriculum, designing features, creating prototypes, and talking to users (rinse and repeat). I used my experience teaching English, German, and Portuguese as foreign languages to develop the app's curriculum.

I currently work at Fit Body App as an Operations Manager. My role is very similar to the above-mentioned tasks (with the exception of the curriculum development). I also oversee all operations within the company, which include day-to-day operations with the app development team, the creative and marketing team, as well as manage third-party partnerships.

Since 2022, I've also been teaching Philosophy, primarily Objectivism, to private students (in English), via the Ayn Rand Center Latin America (in Spanish), and via Objetivismo Brasil (in Portuguese).

I'm currently learning to code, starting with Python, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. My intention is to more effectively implement app features and enhance my communication with the development team.
